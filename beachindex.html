  • Muriwai Beach

Muriwai Beach is a good beach for surfing and bodyboarding. However, there are very strong rip currents and it is best to find out about the dangers of rip currents before you visit Muriwai beach. Muriwai is on the west coast of Auckland above the Waitakere Ranges and is home to a large colony of gannets.

Over a thousand gannets return from Australia every year between August and March.

There is a car park near the beach which leads to a range of viewing platforms that allow visitors to close up views of the colony and the rough coastline.
